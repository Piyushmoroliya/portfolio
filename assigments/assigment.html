*Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj*

1630-1680


Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj or Shivajiraj Shahajiraje Bhonsle (February 19,
1630/February 19, 1627 – April 3, 1680) was the founder of Hindu Swarajya .in 1630 in Shivneri, near
Pune , in the state of Maharashtra . The Maratha kingdom, which he founded in 1674 , ruled until 1818.

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, who fought for the building of a
self-respecting nation, worked hard to unite Hindustan. He conquered many forts in the Konkan region and
gradually brought the entire western India under his rule. Shivaji's bravery and adventure have forever
inspired patriotic rulers.


* Childhood :-

Shahaji, who spent most of his time in wars, was married to Jijabai. Shivaji was born as their son in Shivneri Durga near Pune . He acquired Bangalore as a fiefdom and made it his main military base, and lived with his second wife Sudhabai. Here, Shahaji ruled like an independent king despite being a fiefdom. He minted his own coins. He built a well-furnished house called Gauri Mahal in the fort area. Nandi Hills was already Shahaji's summer capital. Shivaji's first son Sambhaji looked after the administration of the Kolar - Doddaballapur regions.


* Martial Arts :-

Shivaji, who lived in Bangalore until the age of 12, was given his early education by his elder brother, Anna Sambhaji. Later, when Shivaji returned to Shivneri Durga, it was Dadaji Kondadeva who instilled in him full-fledged martial arts. Having mastered swordsmanship, horse riding, and the arts of war, Shivaji had conquered Torandurg by the age of 17 and had begun to take ambitious steps. Shivaji, who had received life values ​​from his mother Jijabai from a young age, was a great devotee of Saint Ramdas. Shivaji, who had received a thorough military education from Dadaji Kondadeva, who was in charge of Shahaji Jagir, gained experience by roaming the forests. Seeing the bravery of the Maval tribe of the Western Ghats, Shivaji gathered them all and started building a strong army. Prominent Maratha leaders also joined hands with them. While his father was living in Bangalore, Jagir, the young Shivaji had discovered the ruins of the unforgettable great empire of Vijayanagara (which had fallen by that time) located near the same area.


Marriage :-

Shivaji, who came to Bangalore with his mother Jijabai and Guru Kondadev, got married around 1640-42. At that time, Shivaji's first marriage was celebrated with Saibai of the Nimbalkar family in Gauri Mahal, which was inside the Bangalore Fort. Shivaji, who came to the important decision of uniting Hindustan, which was shaken by the rule of foreigners, first conquered many forts in the Konkan region. He won many battles with his war skills and founded the Maratha state in 1674.



The family of Shivaji Bhosle, the great ruler of Maharashtra , has its roots in Kannada Nadu. The political alliance between Karnataka and the Marathas began in the first half of the 17th century. In the expansion of the state, the Mughals and the Adil Shahis of Bijapur signed an agreement (1636) and were eager to gain control over the southern part of Karnataka and some parts of Tamil Nadu. Due to this, the Sultans of Bijapur kept conducting expeditions in these regions. In such expeditions, the Maratha chieftains were at the forefront and were responsible for most of the victories. Shahaji Bhosle, son of Maloji Bhosle, was one of such brave Maratha chieftains. Shahaji and Randulla Khan, who were leading the army as per the orders of Vijayapur , faced the army of Kempegowda of Bangalore in December 1638. After a fierce battle, a truce was signed and Kempegowda shifted his capital to Magadi . Bangalore Fort fell into the hands of Vijayapur Sultan. Then, Shahaji Bhonsle received Jahangir of Bangalore from Muhammad Adil Shah. The place where Chhatrapati Shivaji died is Bommasamudra in Chitradurga.
